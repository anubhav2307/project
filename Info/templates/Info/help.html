  • Multivariate Analysis of Protein Polymorphism (MAPP)
    Calculates the difference in physicochemical properties between the wild and mutant amino acids (AA). Larger the deviation, higher is the chance that the mutation causes functional disruption of the protein.
    Prediction: Deleterious/Neutral
    PMID: 15965030

  • Protein Analysis through Evolutionary Relationships (PANTHER)
    An evolutionary based tool which anticipates the function of hypothetical genes based on their evolutionary relationship to experimentally characterized genes with known function.
    Prediction: Pathogenic/Deleterious
    PMID: 20015972

  • Polymorphism Phenotyping (PolyPhen-1)
    Engages a set of empirical rules to determine the functional disruption in presence of mutation.
    Prediction: Deleterious/Neutral
    PMID: 12202775

  • PolyPhen-2
    PolyPhen-2 is different from PolyPhen-1 in terms of features used for prediction and uses eight sequence based and three structure-based AA properties for predicting the functional and structural damage due to AA substitutions.
    Prediction: Deleterious/Neutral
    PMID: 20354512

  • Sorting Intolerant From Tolerant (SIFT)
    An evolutionary based algorithm which measures the impact of AA change on the function of protein based on physical properties and sequence homology.
    Prediction: Deleterious/Neutral
    PMID: 12824425

  • PhD-SNP
    PhD-SNP is a support vector machine classifier-based method that predicts deleterious SNPs using sequence and profile information.: neural network method based on sequence information.
    Prediction: Pathogenic/Benign
    PMID: 16895930

  • Synonymous Non-synonymous Analysis Program (SNAP)
    Based on a set of codon-aligned nucleotide sequences which determine synonymous and non-synonymous substitution rates.
    Prediction: Non-neutral
    PMID: 17526529

  • Site Directed Mutator (SDM)
    Calculates a stability score based on environment-specific AA substitution frequencies within homologous protein families.
    Prediction: Reduced/Increased stability
    PMID: 21593128, 28525590

  • STRUM
    A machine learning approach that integrates graph-based signatures, pharmacophore properties and experimental conditions and uses this information to calculate changes in the stability of protein structure.
    Prediction: Destabilizing/Stabilizing
    PMID: 24281696

  • Mutation Cut-off Scanning Matrix (mCSM)
    A machine learning approach that integrates graph-based signatures, pharmacophore properties and experimental conditions and uses this information to calculate changes in the stability of protein structure.
    Prediction: Destabilizing/Stabilizing
    PMID: 24281696

  • mCSM-lig
    Uses graph-based signatures of wild type structural environment to assess the structural ramifications on drug binding due to mutations in the protein.
    Prediction: Destabilizing/Stabilizing
    PMID: 247384129
